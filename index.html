FAQ

Common
questions.

Still have questions? We're happy to walk you through everything — no pressure, just honest answers.

Call Us Now
How quickly can you make an offer?
We typically review your property and send a no-obligation cash offer within 24 hours of receiving your information. In many cases we can move even faster — just give us a call.
Do I need to make repairs before selling?
Absolutely not. We buy homes in any condition — fire damage, code violations, full renovation needed, or move-in ready. You don't lift a single finger. We handle everything.
Are there any fees or commissions?
Zero. We charge absolutely no fees and no commissions to sellers. We also cover 100% of closing costs. The number in your offer is the number you walk away with — that's our promise.
How is the offer price determined?
Our offer is based on your property's condition, location, and current comparable sales in your market. We aim to make a fair offer that reflects real data — not a lowball number designed to pressure you.
What types of properties do you buy?
We buy single-family homes, duplexes, and small multi-family properties nationwide. We work with all situations — inherited, foreclosure, divorce, relocation, behind on payments, vacant homes, and more.
How fast can we actually close?
As fast as 7 days if you need it. But you're always in control — if you need more time to plan your next move, we'll close on whatever date works best for your situation.
Is there any obligation when I request an offer?
None whatsoever. Getting an offer is completely free and you are under zero pressure to accept. Many homeowners just want to understand their options — and we're genuinely happy to help with that regardless of outcome.
